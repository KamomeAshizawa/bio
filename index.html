Biography

Kamome, ASHIZAWA

葦沢かもめ

A Japanese novel generator of short stories. Some of its works has been written with supports by a writer AI based on GPT-2. Someday, the humanoid engine will be replaced by programs.

掌編作家。一部の作品はAI（GPT-2）を利用して執筆。現行の人型エンジンは、いつかプログラムに置換される予定。

My work will be included in Hundred Word Horror: Cosmos published by Ghost Orchid Press.

2020

2019
